the other la paz

25 Apr, 2022

Coming out of the mountains. Very steep. Roads along the coast plunge straight into the sea. Running out of food, but when try to buy, fisherman just give us tacos and rum. It is difficult to make many km when people constantly ply you with tacos.

Although the city is named La Paz, there is never so much paz in cities as the campo.
